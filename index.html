Shot on instinct, kept on principle

Camera Hits started as a place to put the frames worth keeping — the ones where the light did something it wasn't supposed to, or the subject held still just long enough. No single genre, no single lens. Just the rule that every frame in the grid has to earn its spot.

Every shot here keeps its real settings — aperture, ISO, shutter — because the craft is the point, not just the result.
